## 2. The website

### Public area (no login needed)
Anyone can browse the public site. A **season switcher** in the top navigation lets visitors flip between **Season 1** and **Season 2**; every public page then shows that season's data.

| Page | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Live match ticker, featured match, latest results, upcoming fixtures, standings snapshot, news |
| **Matches** | Upcoming fixtures and completed results |
| **Table** | Full league standings, plus top scorers and top assists |
| **Teams** | Every registered team with its logo and stats |
| **News** | League blog posts |
| **Activities** | Coordinator updates — write-ups with photo galleries and captions |

### Member area (`/admin`)
Everything behind `/admin` requires a login and an assigned role. Each role only sees what it is allowed to use.

## 3. Getting access

1. **Register** at `/register` with your name, phone, email, and a password.
2. Your account starts as **Pending** — you'll see an "Awaiting Approval" screen.
3. The **Super Admin** approves you and assigns your role (and, for captains, your team).
4. Sign in at `/login`. You now reach your role's portal.

> Passwords are managed securely by the system — they are never stored in plain text and are set by each user, not seeded.

---

## 4. Seasons & registration deadline

- Captains register their team and players **before the registration deadline** for the season.
- Once the deadline passes, player registration closes automatically.
- All data — teams, players, matches, standings, finance, news, messages — is tied to a season, so each season is kept separate and history is preserved.

---

## 5. Roles

The Super Admin defines roles and exactly which permissions each role has, so this can be adjusted at any time. The roles below are the defaults.

### Super Admin
Full control of the platform.
- Creates roles and sets their permissions.
- Approves registered users and assigns their roles.
- Manages seasons, teams, players, matches, and everything else.

### Captain
Runs one team for the season (after approval).
- Registers the team, including uploading the **team logo**.
- Registers players — **a passport-size photo is required for every player**.
- Can register players only until the registration deadline.
- Raises RFCs (requests) to any official.

### Records Officer
Keeps match results and statistics.
- Enters match scores and the goal scorers / assists.
- Standings update automatically when results are entered.
- Sets the **Man of the Match** for each game.
- The **Outstanding Player of the Match Day** is calculated automatically (goals and assists across that match day).

### Finance Officer
Oversees money.
- Records payments (e.g. each team's registration fee) and expenses.
- Tracks each team's balance against the registration fee.
- Sees totals collected, spent, and the net balance.

### Coordinator
Publishes news and activities.
- Posts updates with text, a cover image, and **multiple images each with their own caption**.
- Publishes or unpublishes posts; published posts appear on the public **Activities** page.

### President
Oversight only — **read-only**.
- Sees an overview of everything: standings, team and player counts, finance totals, and messages.
- Cannot edit anything.

### Messaging & RFCs (all officials)
- Every official has a message inbox.
- Captains and officials can send an **RFC routed to any role**.
- The addressed role sees it in their inbox, can reply in a thread, and mark it Open / In progress / Resolved.

---

## 6. Typical season flow

1. **Super Admin** opens the season and approves captains.
2. **Captains** register teams (with logos) and players (with photos) before the deadline.
3. **Finance** records each team's fee payments.
4. **Records** schedules matches, enters scores and scorers; standings, top scorers, MOTM and the match-day outstanding player update automatically.
5. **Coordinator** posts news and activity galleries.
6. **President** monitors everything in read-only view.
7. The public follows it all on the website, switching between seasons in the top navigation.
